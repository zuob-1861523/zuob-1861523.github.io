About REAL MADRID

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol (Spanish pronunciation: [reˈal maˈðɾið ˈkluβ ðe ˈfuðβol]; "Royal Madrid Football Club"), commonly known as Real Madrid, is a professional football club based in Madrid, Spain. It is recognized as one of the most sucessful soccer club of 20th century.It is still one of the best soccer club now. The derby between Real Madrid and Bacerlona attract tens of thousands of sports fan to watch every season.
